Chicken Enchilada

Home

This is one of the easiest and tastiest chicken recipe you can make, it may take you a couple of specific ingredients but sure is worthwhile.

Easy Chicken Enchilada Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 can black beans or pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15-ounce) can Southwestern corn, drained
  • ¼ cup salsa, plus more for serving
  • 2 ½ cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 cup green enchilada sauce
  • 4 cups tortilla chips, broken into large pieces
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Mexican Blend cheese

Procedure

follow the following steps for the easy chicken enchilada gourmet you can make at home.

  1. Preheat the oven's broiler on a lower setting if available.
  2. Heat oil in a deep oven-proof skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions and cook until softened, about 2 minutes. Stir in beans, corn, salsa, and chicken until well combined and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. Pour over half of the enchilada sauce and top with half of the chips. Top with remaining sauce, remaining chips, and shredded cheese.
  3. Remove from heat and transfer pan to the preheated oven on the middle rack . Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 3 minutes.
  4. Serve topped with desired toppings and enjoy!
